Open Source & Transparency

At Whispr, we believe that a tool handling your private clipboard data should be completely transparent. Privacy isn't just a promise; it's verifiable through code.

Why Open Source?

1. Trust: You don't have to take our word for it. You can inspect the source code to ensure your data stays local.

2. Security: By being open, the community can help identify and fix potential vulnerabilities faster than any closed team.

3. Collaboration: We want to build the best macOS productivity tool together with the developer community.
